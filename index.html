Two initiatives. One Kingdom vision.

Shepherd's Soul

Caring for those who care for you

America's pastors are enduring a quiet crisis. They're burned out, isolated, and aging — and the churches they serve often don't know how to help. Most existing support focuses on the pastor alone. We work on both sides: renewing pastors and equipping the churches around them.

  • Pastoral renewal retreats for pastors and their spouses
  • Elder board training on the theology and practice of caring for your pastor
  • Sabbatical design and coaching for pastors and church leadership
  • Monthly peer cohorts for mutual encouragement and accountability
  • Congregational seminars on building healthy church cultures
  • Crisis intervention and referral to professional Christian counselors

Café for Christ

A coffee shop. A church. A bridge to the Gospel in Japan.

Japan is the second largest unreached people group in the world. Ninety-five percent of Japanese have never heard the Gospel. Most have never stepped inside a church — but everyone goes to a café.

Café for Christ is a real specialty coffee shop in Saitama City, Japan, sharing space with Mustard Seed Christian Church — a newly planted, gospel-centered congregation. The café operates during the week. The church gathers on Sundays. Same space, two distinct missions, one shared vision: meeting people where they already are.
